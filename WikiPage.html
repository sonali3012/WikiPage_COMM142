1. Printing Services on Campus

Every RIT Dubai student receives a printing credit of 50 AED per semester. Follow these steps to add funds and print your documents efficiently.

Step 1: Add money to your account

  • Navigate to print.ritdubai.edu (must be connected to campus WiFi).
  • Log in using your RIT username and password (same credentials as your email).
  • Click Add Credit and complete payment using a credit card or campus card.
Image Placeholder Screenshot: PaperCut login screen with "Add Credit" highlighted

Step 2: Print your document

  • Locate any printer in the library or computer lab.
  • Swipe your student ID card or manually enter your username.
  • Select Release Print Job from the on-screen menu.
  • Collect your printed document.
Image Placeholder Screenshot: Printer release screen with arrow pointing to "Release Print Job"

Step 3: Troubleshooting common issues

ProblemSolution
Insufficient balanceAdd credit via the web portal (Step 1 above).
Printer not respondingRestart the print job from your laptop or contact the IT Support Desk.
ID card not swipingType your username manually on the printer touchscreen.

Cost per page: 0.20 AED (black and white) / 1 AED (color).

3. University Email Benefits

Your @rit.edu email address is a key that unlocks free and discounted software. Use it to verify your student status on educational platforms.

Top free services (use your RIT email)

  • GitHub Student Developer Pack – Includes Copilot AI, Canva Pro, Namecheap domain, and over 50 development tools. Sign up at education.github.com/pack.
  • Canva for Education – Premium templates, background remover, and 100GB storage. Verify at canva.com/education.
  • Spotify Premium Student – 50% discount on ad-free music and bundled services. Register at spotify.com/student.
Screenshot Placeholder Example: Spotify student verification email in an RIT inbox

General sign-up process

  1. Go to the service's website and locate the student or education verification section.
  2. Enter your full RIT email address (e.g., abc1234@rit.edu).
  3. Check your RIT inbox for a confirmation link.
  4. Click the link to complete verification and enjoy the benefits.

4. Keeta Drone Delivery Near Campus

Keeta operates drone delivery services in the Dubai Silicon Oasis area, including direct service to RIT Dubai campus.

Delivery zone

The drone delivery zone includes: RIT Dubai main building and parking areas, DSO residences (Cedre, Golden Sands, etc.), and Silicon Central Mall.

Map Placeholder Keeta app map showing the drone delivery zone with RIT Dubai marked

How to place an order

  1. Download the Keeta app (iOS or Android).
  2. Enter your delivery address.
  3. Look for items marked with a drone icon.
  4. Complete your order – the drone will land at the nearest drone port.
  5. Walk to the drone port and enter the pickup code displayed in your app.
Screenshot Placeholder Keeta app order screen with the drone delivery icon highlighted

Important notes

  • Drone delivery fee: 5 AED (cheaper than standard car delivery, which costs 8-10 AED).
  • Maximum weight per order: 2.5 kg.
  • Available hours: 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.

Need assistance? Visit the IT Support Desk in Building 1, ground floor, or email help@ritdubai.edu.
