RIT Dubai Student Tech Guide

A comprehensive technology resource for new and current students

This guide provides step-by-step instructions to help RIT Dubai students access essential campus technology services. From adding printing credit to releasing print jobs and troubleshooting common issues, this resource is designed to make your experience smooth and efficient.

RIT Dubai Printing Guide

1. Printing Services on Campus

Every RIT Dubai student receives a printing credit of 90 AED per semester. Follow these steps to add funds and print your documents efficiently.

How to Add Money for Printing

Step 1:Choose your payment method

  • 1. Deposit a cheque
  • 2. Pay online using a secured card payment link
  • 3.Pay in person at the Finance Office (debit/credit card)

1. Cheque Payment

  • Deposit your cheque into the RIT Dubai Finance Office bank account
  • Email the deposit slip to dubaiar@rit.edu
  • Include your full name and University ID or application number

2. Online Card Payment

  • Email dubaiar@rit.edu with your details
  • Include: name, University ID, fee description, and amount
  • Receive a secured payment link (valid for 24 hours)
  • Complete payment online

3. International Transfers

  • Use IBAN for international transfers
  • Payments must be in AED (UAE dirhams)

How to Print Documents

Step 2: Print your document

  • Download the MyQ X app to your device from the app store
  • Save the document to your device such as the Files app on Apple devices
  • Go to any printer in the library or computer labs
  • Add paper to the printer if there is none, it is usually at the bottom of the printer
  • If no paper is available, ask the security guards
  • Scan the QR code or enter your username
  • Upload the document to the MyQ X app
  • Choose if you want the document in black and white or color
  • Choose how many copies you want to print
  • Select Release Print Job from the screen
  • Collect your printed document
MyQ X Application

Step 3: Troubleshooting common issues

Problem Solution
Insufficient balance Add credit via the web portal (Step 1 above)
Printer not responding Restart the print job or contact IT Support
QR code not working Manually enter your ID on the printer

Cost per page: 0.50 AED (black & white) / 1 AED (color)



RIT Dubai Student ID card guide

2. Student ID Benefits – Save Money

Your student ID can help you with a lot of different things, from getting a student discount for Spotify Premium to being able to eat at certain restaurants for free. Always carry it with you or keep a digital photo on your phone.

Confirmed discounts near campus

Wingstop Frequently have promotions for students (like 10 wings for AED 25) Show ID or a clear photo of your ID.

The International Student Card (ISIC) & NOL

Access 50% on all bus rides by getting a student NOL card. By paying just an extra 25 AED at checkout for your student NOL card, you can also avail an international student card named the "ISIC Student ID."

The ISIC card not only identifies you as a student in the UAE but also internationally, allowing you to have access towards a plethora of discounts such as:

  • Flights: Special student fares for Emirates and Oman Air.
  • Travel: Save up to 20% with Booking.com.
  • Subscriptions: YouTube Premium at half the price.
  • Tech: 15% off on any new Huawei products.
  • Apparel: 15% off on all Under Armour items.
Pro Tip: Save a high-resolution photo of your student ID on your phone. Many merchants accept the digital version if you forget the physical card.

If you have a ISIC Student ID, you can access it through the ISIC app.

Keeta Drone Delivery Guide

4. Keeta Drone Delivery Near Campus

Keeta now offers drone delivery in the Dubai Silicon Oasis area, including direct service to RIT Dubai. This guide walks you through your first drone order, from downloading the app to picking up your package at the drone port.

What you need to know before you start

  • Delivery fee: Only 5 AED (car delivery costs 8-10 AED).
  • Max weight: 2.5 kg per order.
  • Hours: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM daily.
  • Zone: RIT Dubai campus compound, DSO.

Step-by-step guide to using Keeta drone delivery

Step 1: Download the Keeta app

Go to the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, search for "Keeta Drone", and install the app.


Step 2: Sign up or log in

Open the app and register using your phone number or email. Verify the OTP sent to your device.


Step 3: Enter your delivery address

Tap the address bar at the top of the home screen. Type "RIT Dubai" or your exact building. The app will check if drone delivery is available at that address.


Pro Tip – Check drone eligibility early
The drone icon only appears for eligible addresses and restaurants. Enter your address first before browsing.

Step 4: Look for the drone icon

Only items that can be delivered by drone show a small drone icon next to them. Browse restaurants and look for the icon.


Step 5: Add items to your cart and check out

Select your items, go to the cart, and confirm that the delivery method says "Drone Delivery (5 AED)". Complete payment.


Pro Tip – Save on fees
Drone delivery is cheaper (5 AED) than car delivery (8-10 AED). Use it for small, urgent orders to save money.

Step 6: Wait for the "Ready for pickup" notification

You will see a live map of the drone's flight. When it lands at the nearest drone port, the app sends a push notification with a 6-digit pickup code.


Step 7: Walk to the drone port

The notification tells you exactly where the port is (e.g., "Near Block E"). Your drone port should look like the picture below.


Step 8: Enter your pickup code and collect your order

On the port's screen, tap "Pick up order", then enter the 6-digit code from your app. The compartment will open – take your package and close the door. Once the drone arrives at the Drone Port and the order has been completed, the invoice will be sent to the email address you provided during registration.


Step 9: Confirm receipt in the app

Tap "Order received" and rate your experience (optional). Enjoy your delivery!


Troubleshooting common issues

ProblemSolution
No drone icon appears on any item Drone delivery is only available from select partners within the DSO zone. Make sure your address is set to "RIT Dubai". Try moving closer to campus.
App says "Drone not available at this address" The delivery zone is limited. If you are outside the zone, use car delivery instead or walk to a known drone port (In front of the cafeteria or near Block E).
I missed the pickup notification – the drone left Orders are held at the port for only 10 minutes. If you miss it, the order will be returned and refunded automatically. Re-order and stay near the port.
The port screen says "Invalid code" Make sure you are using the 6-digit pickup code from "My Orders" – not the order number. Tap "Resend code" in the app to get a new one.
The compartment door is stuck / won't open Do not force it. Contact Keeta support on their customer service hotline(+97144986429). They will remotely release the door or issue a refund.

Quick reference card

  • Delivery point: Rochester Institute of Technology – Dubai Drone Port Canteen entrance, Rochester Institute of Technology – Dubai, Dubai Silicon Oasis
  • Delivery fee: 5 AED (drone) vs 8-10 AED (car).
  • Max weight: 2.5 kg.
  • Operating hours: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM, 7 days a week.
  • Pickup code expiry: 10 minutes from landing.
  • For any more FAQs:.Go to the Keeta Drone support page.

Final Pro Tip – Keep your phone handy
The pickup code expires quickly. Walk to the port as soon as you get the notification, and have the app open on the "My Orders" screen.
